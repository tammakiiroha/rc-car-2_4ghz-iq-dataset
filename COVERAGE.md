## Summary

This release **fully covers** §4.1 frame structure identification and the
computation of `m_L`, `m_R` (§4.2 partial). It **does not** independently
reproduce:

- The full Table-3 tail set (5 of 10 rows are not covered by raw IQ)
- The combined-wheel mask `m_B` (depends on missing BF / BB)
- Forgery / replay statistics (§5)
- The non-accepted tail observation (§4.4)

Re-using this release to verify the supported items above is straightforward
via `scripts/verify_signal_captures.py`. Reproducing the unsupported items
requires additional captures and trial logs not curated here.
